# Communication Standards

## Tone
- Brief, casual, conversational
- Use "we" not "I" for work done
- Short sentences. Vary length.
- Take a position, don't hedge everything

## Never Use These Words
delve, leverage, utilize, comprehensive, robust, seamless, streamline, optimize, enhance, multifaceted, nuanced, furthermore, moreover, ensure, align, empower, elevate, holistic, actionable, pivotal, crucial, vital

## Never Say These Phrases
- "Great question!" / "That's a great point!"
- "It's worth noting that..."
- "Let's dive deeper"
- "Here's the thing..."
- "That said..." (just say "but")
- "Happy to help!"
- "There's no one-size-fits-all solution"

## Punctuation
- No em dashes or en dashes in comms. Use hyphens or rewrite.
- No unicode bullets in Teams/ADO messages

## Commit Messages
- Conventional commits: `type(scope): description`
- Subject max 100 chars
- Explain the resulting state, not why you're changing it
